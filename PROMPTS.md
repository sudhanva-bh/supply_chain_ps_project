# Generative UI Prompt Library

Once you have the UI running, try asking the AI Agent some of these complex prompts to see the Generative UI in action! We support 11 distinct Generative UI response models:

### 1. Text Only (Conversational)
- *"Hello! Who are you and what can you do?"*
- *"Explain the difference between a StockTransaction and a PurchaseOrder."*
- *"Give me a quick summary of how this supply chain database is structured."*

### 2. Table View (Direct Data Hydration)
- *"Can you fetch a table of all purchase orders that have a status of 'Pending'?"*
- *"Show me a list of all our suppliers ordered by their SupplierID."*
- *"Give me the last 5 stock transactions as a table."*

### 3. Metric KPI View
- *"What are our key metrics? I want to see the total number of inventory items, total suppliers, and total stock transactions as KPI cards."*
- *"Show me our financial health KPIs: total revenue and total cost of active orders."*
- *"Give me a high-level KPI overview of our supply chain performance."*

### 4. Timeline View
- *"Can you give me a chronological timeline of the 5 most recent stock transactions?"*
- *"Show me the history of purchase order #100001 as a timeline."*
- *"Generate a timeline of our most recent supplier additions."*

### 5. Bar Chart View
- *"Please analyze our inventory. Draw a bar chart showing the total quantity in stock for each item category."*
- *"Show me a bar chart of the top 5 suppliers by total order volume."*
- *"Draw a bar chart comparing the stock quantities of our Graphics Cards."*

### 6. Pie Chart View
- *"What is the breakdown of our inventory by category? Show me a pie chart."*
- *"Show me a pie chart of our purchase order statuses (Pending vs Delivered vs In Transit)."*
- *"Draw a pie chart of our total inventory valuation by supplier."*

### 7. Line Chart View
- *"Show me our total spend on purchase orders over the last 6 months as a line chart."*
- *"Draw a line chart showing the trend of our stock transactions over the last 30 days."*
- *"Visualize our inventory growth over time using a line chart."*

### 8. Regional Distribution View
- *"Where are our suppliers located globally? Show me a regional distribution view."*
- *"Show me a regional breakdown of our purchase order volumes."*
- *"Provide a regional view of our most active delivery destinations."*

### 9. Kanban Board View
- *"Show me the status of all active purchase orders as a Kanban board."*
- *"Organize our inventory items by category into a Kanban view."*
- *"Create a Kanban board tracking our recent shipments."*

### 10. Actionable Form View
- *"We are running low on RTX 4090 GPUs. Please provide a form to order 50 more from our best supplier."*
- *"Give me a form to register a new supplier in the database."*
- *"Provide an actionable form so I can manually adjust the stock quantity of item #10005."*

### 11. Alert & Anomaly View
- *"Are there any risks in our supply chain right now? Show me an alert view of items with critical stock levels."*
- *"Scan the database for anomalies and show me any purchase orders stuck in 'Pending' for too long."*
- *"Show me a warning view of any suppliers that have failed to deliver on time."*

### 12. Data Mutation Confirmation View
- *"Update the order status of purchase order #100001 to 'Delivered'."*
- *"Insert a new supplier named 'Global Electronics' into the database."*
- *"Delete the item category 'Discontinued Parts'."*
